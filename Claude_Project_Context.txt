## PRIORITIZATION METHODOLOGY

Composite Priority Score Formula:
```
Priority Score =
  (Traffic_Score × 40%) +
  (Staleness_Score × 30%) +
  (CSAT_Score × 20%) +
  (Never_Reviewed_Bonus × 10%)

Where:
- Traffic_Score = % of Traffic × 1,000,000
- Staleness_Score = DaysSinceReview / 365
- CSAT_Score = 100 - Content_SAT (inverted)
- Never_Reviewed_Bonus = 10 points if never reviewed
```

Tier Definitions:
- Tier 1: Traffic > 0.0001% AND (Days > 180 OR Never Reviewed)
- Tier 2: Traffic 0.00001-0.0001% AND (Days > 730 OR Never Reviewed)
- Tier 3: CSAT = 0 AND Traffic > 0.000001%
- Tier 4: Get Started, Landing, or Navigation content types
